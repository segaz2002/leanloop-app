# LeanLoop

## What This Is

A beginner-friendly iOS/Android fitness app that combines a **3-day full body lifting plan** with a **macro-lite fat loss loop** (protein + steps + weekly check-in). It adapts week-to-week and is sold as a monthly subscription.

## Core Value

**Beginners get a plan they can follow and weekly adjustments that keep them progressing.**
